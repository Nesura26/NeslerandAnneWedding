Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a plus one?
Due to limited seating, only guests named on the invitation are invited. We'd like to keep our wedding solemn & intimate with the most important people in our lives. We'd kindly request NO ADDITIONAL GUESTS OR PLUS ONES.
Can I bring kids?
To maintain a solemn and intimate atmosphere, our celebration will be an ADULTS-ONLY event. Only children who are part of the entourage are invited to share in our celebration. Thank you for your understanding.
Can I leave early?
We have lovingly planned this celebration over the past two (2) years, and it would mean so much to have you stay and celebrate with us until the end of the program.
What if I miss the RSVP deadline?
If we don't receive your RSVP by the specified date, you will not be able to attend. If you were to show up without submitting an RSVP, there will be no seat for you. If, in case, you need to update your RSVP, simply send us a message.
