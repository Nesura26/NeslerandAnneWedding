Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring kids?
Due to limited seating, only guests named on the invitation are invited. We'd like to keep our wedding solemn & intimate with the most important people in our lives. We'd kindly request NO ADDITIONAL GUESTS OR PLUS ONES. Please refer to the number of guests allotted for you. Thank you for understanding.
Can I bring a plus one?
To maintain a solemn and intimate atmosphere, our celebration will be an ADULTS-ONLY event. Only children who are part of the entourage are invited to share in our celebration. Thank you for your understanding.
Can I leave early?
We have lovingly planned this celebration over the past two years, and it would mean so much to have you stay and celebrate with us until the end of the program. We hope you'll be with us for every moment — especially the fireworks and same-day edit!
What if I miss the RSVP deadline?
If we don't receive your RSVP by the specified date, you will not be able to attend — there will be no seat reserved. If you need to update your RSVP, simply send us a message. We appreciate your promptness!
